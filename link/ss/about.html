About

Shonali Sabherwal is a celebrity macrobiotic nutritionist. She is known for her brand Soulfood, which is about the macrobiotic approach to lifestyle and diet. Under this brand she endorses all products which include her consultations, meals & ready-to-eat products.[1] She has actively been a part of the macrobiotic culture in India since 2006. She is the author of the books – 'The Beauty Diet' published in 2012, and 'The Love Diet' published in February 2014.

Shonali has authored two books: ‘The Beauty Diet’ and ‘The Love Diet’. Both these books focus on creating a healthy life with the Macrobiotic approach and have diets related to health, beauty, creating love in one's life, and maintaining a calm mind.

In January 2012, she authored 'The Beauty Diet' which was published by Random House. The Beauty Diet' focuses mainly on the basics of the Macrobiotic approach to health and beauty. It provides information on detox diets and contains testimonials from celebrity clients.

‘The Love Diet' published in 2014 focuses on concepts like self-love, compassionate love, and love between the sexes and links this to a healthy approach to food that can help minimise complexities in relationships with oneself an with others, as well as enhance all shades of love.

After professionally entering the field Macrobiotics in 2008, Shonali has extended her services from counselling to other allied fields. She started a catering service of macrobiotic health meals and macrobiotic consultations on diet and lifestyle. She also conducts cooking classes and produces ready-to-eat snacks and products.

Shonali's clientele includes people from various walks of life. She counsels corporate professionals, lawyers, homemakers, aspiring models, film stars and airline crew. She also advises patients suffering from disorders like diabetes, polycystic ovary disorder, thyroid, cancer, skin conditions and other health problems/conditions.Shonali has counselled well-known film personalities of Bollywood – Indian film industry. These include Hema Malini, Katrina Kaif, Esha Deol, Jacqueline Fernandez,Neha Dhupia, Shekhar Kapur, Kabir Bedi, Dalip Tahil, Vikram Bhatt and others.
